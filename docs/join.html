Join the fight to stop COVID

We are a not-for-profit organization who are passionate about making an impact on the world. We foster a great learning and mentorship environment for people to grow and use their skills to make an impact.

People

Human Resources Partner

This person will contribute on a part-time volunteer basis and collaborate closely with the management team to help oversee the creation and refinement of job competencies/descriptions, and participate in talent acquisition efforts and advise applicant screening.

Youth Ambassador

We are seeking volunteers who can establish a campaign so that as many people as possible in their local or regional area are regularly using Virufy's web and smartphone apps to provide their cough data.

Project Manager

We are seeking a project manager who will work closely with the development team in order to define a product roadmap, manage development progress and align with stakeholders.

Business/Finance Manager

This person will contribute on a part-time volunteer basis and collaborate closely with the management and development team to help manage the overall business, product, and finances.

Engineering

UX Researcher / Designer

We are looking for a UX researcher/ UX designer passionate about designing user-friendly web and mobile apps. You will be collaborating with our engineers to develop and maintain high quality mobile-friendly applications to be used by millions of people.

Web App Developer

We are looking for a qualified Web app developer to join our Engineering team. Our team of volunteers develop and maintain high quality web applications, which could be seen by hundreds of millions of people. If you’re passionate about frontend web development and helping people around the world, we would like to meet you. As a web developer, you’ll collaborate with internal teams to redesign our website applications, while in a fast-paced environment.

Android App Developer

We are looking for a qualified Android app developer to join our Engineering team. You will be collaborating with our engineers to develop and maintain high quality mobile-friendly applications, which could be used by hundreds of millions of people.

Twilio Developer

We are looking for a qualified Twilio developer to join our Engineering team. If you’re passionate about developing our Twilio backend and helping people around the world, we would like to meet you. As a Twilio developer, you’ll collaborate with internal teams to develop and periodically update our Twilio infrastructure in a fast-paced environment.

AI Engineer

Our team of volunteers works with machine learning, deep learning, and signal analysis for audio analysis in a medical setting. We welcome volunteers with experience in these domains.

Health AI Mentors

Our team of volunteers works with machine learning, deep learning, and signal analysis for audio, video, and textual analysis in a medical setting. We also welcome the expertise of more established people within the field of AI in an advisor or mentorship role.

Medical

Clinical Research Coordinator (CRC)

Seeking individuals to support in coordinating clinical activities. The CRC will be involved in preparing research protocols, reporting, informed consent, conduct of research, safeguarding PHI, and any general administrative matters.

Marketing

Video Editor

We are looking for a video editor to contribute on a part-time volunteer basis who is passionate about editing digital content and creating graphics/illustrations for outreach and marketing purposes.

Legal

Data Privacy Manager

This person will contribute on a part-time volunteer basis and collaborate closely with the management and development team to help manage the overall data privacy concerns internally, potentially under mentorship from senior licensed lawyers.
